About Olamide.

I am Akintoye Oluwatobiloba Olamide! I'm very enthusiastic about tech, and I put my best and beyond into everything I am committed to doing. What I love most about tech is teaching other young Nigerians; my dream is to make sure every young Nigerian has an idea of how to code and to help those with a greater interest in technology break into tech careers. I'm passionate about seeing my country contribute to the technological development of the world, and I love working towards that goal. Being a UI/UX designer has also made it easy for me to travel further along this path, because of my ability to design every bit of my ideas before I start coding. My tools include HTML/CSS, Ruby, Ruby on Rails, Postgresql, MySQL, SQLite3, JavaScript, NodeJS, and React. I am also experienced with remote pair programming and have strong communication skills. I love talking with other devs/designers.
